Persian

Persian cats are known for their luxurious long coats, flat faces, and gentle personalities. These elegant felines are calm, affectionate, and enjoy a relaxed indoor lifestyle. With their expressive eyes and quiet demeanor, they make wonderful companions for those who can commit to regular grooming and attentive care.

  • Life Span: 10-15 years
  • Weight: 7-12 pounds
  • Height: 9-10 inches
  • Main Disease: Respiratory issues due to their flat faces

    • Siamese

    Siamese cats are known for their striking blue almond-shaped eyes, short coat with color points on their ears, face, paws, and tail. They are affectionate, vocal, and social, often forming strong bonds with their owners. Playful and intelligent, Siamese cats enjoy interactive games and thrive on companionship and attention.

  • Life Span: 12-20 years
  • Weight: 6-14 pounds
  • Height: 8-10 inches
  • Main Disease: Dental problems and respiratory issues

    • Maine Coon

    Maine Coon cats are known for their impressive size, tufted ears, bushy tails, and friendly nature. They exhibit intelligence, playfulness, and adapt well to various environments. With a gentle and sociable demeanor, Maine Coons often form strong bonds with their families and get along well with children and other pets.

  • Life Span: 12-15 years
  • Weight: 10-25 pounds
  • Height: 10-16 inches
  • Main Disease: Hypertrophic Cardiomyopathy (heart condition)

    • Ragdoll

    Ragdoll cats are known for their gentle and affectionate nature. They thrive on human companionship and often go limp when picked up, hence the name "Ragdoll." These large, semi-longhaired cats have striking blue eyes, soft fur, and a tendency to follow their owners around. They are calm, friendly, and great for indoor living.

  • Life Span: 12-17 years
  • Weight: 10-20 pounds
  • Height: 9-11 inches
  • Main Disease: Feline hypertrophic cardiomyopathy

    • Bengal

    Bengal cats are known for their lively and playful nature. With their striking leopard-like spots and sleek coats, they exude elegance. Intelligent and curious, they enjoy interactive play and require mental stimulation. Their affectionate yet independent demeanor makes them a unique and captivating addition to any household.

  • Life Span: 12-16 years
  • Weight: 8-15 pounds
  • Height: 9-10 inches
  • Main Disease: Hypertrophic Cardiomyopathy and Patellar Luxation

    • Sphynx

    The Sphynx cat is a unique breed known for its hairless appearance, wrinkled skin, and large ears. Despite lacking fur, they're friendly, affectionate, and seek warmth from human companionship. Their curious and playful nature makes them delightful companions. Regular bathing is required due to their lack of fur, fostering a strong bond between owner and cat.

  • Life Span: 8-14 years
  • Weight: 6-12 pounds
  • Height: 8-10 inches
  • Main Disease: Skin issues due to lack of fur

    • Scottish Fold

    Scottish Fold cats are known for their distinctive folded ears that give them an endearing appearance. They are gentle, affectionate, and often seek human companionship. These cats have a calm and adaptable nature, making them great indoor companions. Their sweet demeanor and charming look make them a favorite among cat enthusiasts.

  • Life Span: 11-15 years
  • Weight: 6-13 pounds
  • Height: 7-10 inches
  • Main Disease: Osteochondrodysplasia (cartilage and bone disorders)

    • Russian Blue

    Russian Blue cats are known for their striking silver-blue coat, captivating green eyes, and gentle demeanor. They exhibit intelligence, often forming strong bonds with their owners. Reserved with strangers, they thrive in calm environments. Playful yet poised, these cats bring a touch of elegance and companionship to any home.

  • Life Span: 15-20 years
  • Weight: 7-12 pounds
  • Height: 8-10 inches
  • Main Disease: None specific, but they can be prone to obesity

    • Abyssinian

    Abyssinian cats are known for their captivating appearance and lively personalities. With a sleek, ticked coat in warm tones, they exude elegance. These intelligent and curious felines are energetic, forming strong bonds with their families. Their playful nature and inquisitive demeanor make them delightful companions for those who appreciate an active and engaging pet.

  • Life Span: 9-15 years
  • Weight: 6-10 pounds
  • Height: 8-10 inches
  • Main Disease: Gingivitis and dental issues

    • British Shorthair

    British Shorthair cats are known for their charming and easygoing personalities. They exude a calm demeanor and enjoy lounging around the house. Affectionate but not overly demanding, they form strong bonds with their families. Their round faces and plush coats add to their adorable and dignified appearance.

  • Life Span: 12-20 years
  • Weight: 9-18 pounds
  • Height: 12-14 inches
  • Main Disease: Hypertrophic Cardiomyopathy (heart condition)
